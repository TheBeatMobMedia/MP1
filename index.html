About Me

Two decades deep in the music industry, I specialize in metadata optimization, digital asset management, and creative strategy. I'm the founder of The Beat Mob, an independent creative and production brand built to serve as a full-stack content hub for artists—handling everything from beat licensing and artwork to metadata submission and campaign rollout. My metadata work ensures accurate royalty tracking, seamless platform ingestion, and discoverability across DSPs.

  • Cross-functional Communication (Artists, A&Rs, Engineers, Developers)

  • Metadata Encoding & QC (DDEX, CWR, ISRC, UPC, WAV tagging)
  • Digital Distribution Prep (DistroKid, TuneCore, LabelGrid, and platform-ready metadata formats)
  • Audio Engineering (Vocal production, mixing/mastering)
  • Visual & Motion Branding (Cover art, social rollout assets)
  • Catalog Management & Asset Delivery (CSV, XML, Excel)
  • Creative Campaign Execution (EPK, one-sheets, reels)
